Description

This is one of the integrations for the Don Quixote project aiming to combine flight and water travel. The bicopter is a two-rotor flying machine that will pivot its propellers vertically for flight or horizontally for water travel. There has been a successful Chinook bicopter of a similar design but we are taking it a step further by implementing underwater functionality to it.
