Getting Involved

No experience or well-versed in robotics, we welcome everyone to join us in our activities. Students of any major and people of the community can join our group to learn more about robotics by working on projects and participating in workshops.

Join Us

  1. Let us know more about you by filling out the New Member Form
  2. Join us on OrgSync
  3. Attend one of our meetings so that we know who you are. Email us at robotics@pdx.edu and ask us when our meetings take place.
  4. Let us know how often you're available on a weekly basis. We will use this information to plan meetings and events for members.

Chat with us on Discord

Join our Discord chat server and come talk with members of the society!
